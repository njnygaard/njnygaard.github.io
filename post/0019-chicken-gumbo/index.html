Chicken Gumbo

14 Jan 2018, 10:17
chicken / gumbo / soup / stew

This is my standard Gumbo. Alton Brown posted a recipe for inspiration, but I really just do whatever I feel like. I’m not cooking for southerners, so I can deviate from the classics. I’m not about to go hunting for fil√® powder.

Ingredient Quantity
Red Chili Flakes 2 Teaspoons
Onion, Yellow, Chopped 1 Cup
Garlic, Clove 6
Peppers, Bell, Medium, Fresh 2
Basil Leaves 5
Flour, Wheat, All Purpose 2 Tablespoons
Butter, salted 2 Tablespoons
Tomatos, Diced No Salt 4 Cups
Celery, Diced 1 Cup
Chicken Stock, Organic 2 Cups
Okra 4 Cups

Make your roux first with the red ingredients.

Then throw the green ingredients in the pot and stir and soften them up a bit. Just until fragrant.

Then dump you your blue ingredients in there and simmer till we’re all friends.

You’ll notice there is no protein here. I like to cook all of that on the side and weigh it before serving. If I had a rough gym day, more. If I sat on my ass and blogged, even more.
